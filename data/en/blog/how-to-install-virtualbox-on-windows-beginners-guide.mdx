## Why VirtualBox?

VirtualBox stands out as a versatile and user-friendly virtualization tool, allowing you to run multiple operating systems on a single computer without any hassle. It’s a powerful solution for developers, testers, and anyone curious about exploring different operating systems within their familiar Windows environment.
